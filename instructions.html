Instructions
  • Defeat the dragon!
  • Each dragon has a set Armor Class, that you need to beat to win.
  • Click on the d10's at the bottom of the screen to find the value of their roll.
  • You only have 3 guesses, so guess wisely!
  • If you roll higher than the dragons armor class, you win!
  • If you don't, you lose!
  • Happy playing!
